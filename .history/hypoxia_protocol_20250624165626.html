Hemoglobin Tool Hypoxia Protocol

Hypoxia + Isocapnic Bag Protocol

Why Use This Protocol?

This protocol is designed for endurance athletes, biohackers, and individuals aiming to increase their red blood cell (RBC) mass, improve oxygen transport, and support liver function through enhanced ceruloplasmin (Cp) production. By combining controlled hypoxia sessions with intermittent isocapnic breathing, this approach:

  • Stimulates erythropoietin (EPO) production and reticulocyte response
  • Enhances red blood cell mass for improved endurance
  • Supports ceruloplasmin output and iron mobilization
  • Maintains cerebral perfusion and reduces central fatigue during hypoxia
  • Enables higher tolerance for 2x/day hypoxia blocks without sympathetic overload

The Protocol

This protocol is synthesized from these studies and field use at elite sports training centers (e.g., AIS, INSEP).
